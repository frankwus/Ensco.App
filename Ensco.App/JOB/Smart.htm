What Establish what exactly happened Where Determine the exact location of where the problem occurred When Determine the details of when the problem occurred Who Establish people present when it happened, that are involved or have the problem How Determine the specifics of how the problem occurred, but do not perform causal analysis at this stage Why The “Why” may be established using the Ensco 3by5 or during a TapRooT® investigation S.M.A.R.T.E.R. CAPAs Specific • Directly address the nonconformity or unplanned event (What) • State specific requirements and constraints (How) • State applicable location(s) (Where) Measurable • Specify what must be accomplished to close the action • Specify how progress will be measured Assignable • Be assigned to a specific person (Who) • Be assigned to personnel capable of completing the action Realistic • Be achieved given available resources Timely • Have a specific due date given a reasonable timeframe to complete (When) Effective • Evaluate performance and progress Reviewed • Review for lessons learned and provide feedback
